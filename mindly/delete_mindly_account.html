How to Delete Your Mindly Account

To delete your Mindly account, please follow these simple steps:

  1. Log in to your Mindly account
  2. Go to Settings (usually found in the top-right menu)
  3. Scroll down to find "Delete Account" option
  4. Click "Delete Account" and confirm your choice

Note: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted.

If you need assistance, please contact support at support@mindly.com
